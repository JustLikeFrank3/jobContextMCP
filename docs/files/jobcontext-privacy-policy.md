## The short version

Here is the plain-English gist. It is not a substitute for the full policy below, but it is the honest summary:

- We collect what you put into jobContext (your career stuff) plus basic account info from your Microsoft sign-in. That is it.
- We never sell your data, never use it for ads, and never use it to train our own models.
- To build resumes, cover letters, and prep, we send the relevant parts to AI providers (Anthropic and OpenAI) that do not keep your data or train on it.
- The wellbeing check-in is opt-in. If you never log a check-in, there is no mood data at all. You can delete any or all of it whenever you want.
- Anything you record about other people (recruiters, referrals) stays your private address book. We do not market to them and we do not sell it.
- You can see, export, and delete everything, including your entire account, at any time.

This Privacy Policy explains what information jobContext ("we," "us," the "Service") collects, why we collect it, how we handle it, and the choices you have. jobContext is a career-memory service that gives AI assistants persistent context across your job search. The Service is currently offered as an invite-only beta to users in the United States.

If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the Service.

## 1. Who this policy covers

This policy covers people who sign in to and use jobContext (our "users"). It also explains how we handle information a user records about other people, such as recruiters or referral contacts. If you are one of those third parties and have a question about your information, contact us at admin@jobcontext.ai, though note that the user who recorded your information controls it and is your first point of contact.

## 2. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the Service.

**Account and identity information.** You sign in with Microsoft (Microsoft Entra ID). When you do, we receive basic identity details from Microsoft to create and secure your account: your name, email address, your Microsoft Entra object ID, and your tenant ID. We do not receive your Microsoft password.

**Career information you provide.** This is the core of the Service and includes whatever you choose to add: resume content, work history, skills, accomplishments and metrics, personal stories and anecdotes, job descriptions, applications, interview notes, outreach messages, and similar materials.

**Contacts you record about other people.** As part of managing a job search, you can record information about other individuals, for example recruiters, hiring managers, or referral contacts. This may include their name, employer, role, and your notes about your relationship with them. This functions as your professional address book. See Section 4 for how we handle it and your responsibilities.

**Optional wellbeing check-ins.** jobContext includes an optional mental-health check-in feature. This information is created only when you choose to record a check-in. If you never record a check-in, no mood or wellbeing data is collected or stored about you. When you do record one, we store the mood label, energy rating, and any note you write, for the sole purpose of showing you your own history and trends. We treat this as sensitive personal information and handle it with extra care. See Section 5.

**Usage and technical information.** To keep the Service running and improve reliability, we collect limited technical data such as feature and tool usage, timestamps, error logs, and basic request metadata.

We do not collect payment information during the beta.

## 3. How we use information

We use information to:

- Provide the Service and your account, including authentication and per-user data isolation.
- Generate the outputs you ask for, such as tailored resumes, cover letters, interview preparation, and message drafts.
- Maintain, secure, debug, and improve the Service.
- Communicate with you about your account or the beta.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your personal information for advertising. We do not use your content to train our own models.

## 4. Contacts you record about other people

Information you record about third parties is processed by us only to provide the Service to you. We do not market to these individuals, sell their information, or use it for any purpose other than operating your account.

You are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful and appropriate basis to record information about other people, and for using that information responsibly. You should not record information you are not permitted to hold. If a third party asks us to remove their information, we may direct them to you as the controller of that information, or, where required by law, act on the request directly.

## 5. Sensitive information (wellbeing check-ins)

The optional wellbeing check-in feature can involve information about your mental or emotional state, which is treated as sensitive personal information under applicable law.

- It is entirely opt-in. The data exists only because you chose to record a check-in.
- It is used only to display your own history and trends back to you.
- It is never sold, never used for advertising, and never used to train any model.
- It is stored within your isolated per-user data, subject to the same security and deletion controls as the rest of your data.
- You can delete any check-in, or all of them, at any time.

## 6. How your information is processed and who we share it with

We share information only with the service providers ("sub-processors") we use to operate the Service, and only as needed.

**AI processing.** To generate tailored outputs, we send relevant portions of your data to third-party AI providers (currently Anthropic and OpenAI) through their commercial APIs. Under the API terms we use, these providers process the data only to return a result for your request, do not retain it after the request, and do not use it to train their models.

**Hosting and authentication.** The Service runs on Microsoft Azure, which provides hosting and sign-in (Microsoft Entra ID).

**Current sub-processors:**

| Sub-processor | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Microsoft Azure | Hosting, infrastructure, authentication | United States |
| Anthropic | AI text generation (commercial API, no training, no retention) | United States |
| OpenAI | AI text generation (commercial API, no training, no retention) | United States |

We may otherwise disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transfer, in which case we will provide notice as required.

## 7. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in Microsoft Azure data centers located in the United States. Each account's data is held in a separate, isolated per-user store so that one user's data is not accessible to another.

## 8. How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can delete individual items or your entire account at any time. When you delete your account, we remove your data from active systems. Residual copies in routine backups are purged on our standard backup cycle, and we do not restore deleted data from backups except as needed to recover the system as a whole.

## 9. Your choices and rights

You can:

- **Access and review** the information in your account at any time through the Service.
- **Correct or update** your information directly.
- **Export** your data.
- **Delete** individual items, categories of data (including all wellbeing check-ins), or your entire account.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act. To exercise any right not available directly in the Service, contact us at admin@jobcontext.ai. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

## 10. Security

We protect your data with measures including Microsoft Entra ID authentication and per-user data isolation. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your information and to limit access to it. If we become aware of a breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by law.

## 11. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Service or by email and update the effective date above. Your continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
